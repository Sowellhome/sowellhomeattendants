Our Story & Our Mission

Sowell Home Attendants was founded on a simple belief: that every person deserves to age with grace, dignity, and the comfort of home.

“We started this agency because we believe home is where healing happens. When people are surrounded by familiar walls, their routines, and people who genuinely care — they do better. Our mission is to make that possible for every family we serve.” — Sowell Home Attendants, Founding Vision
Who We Are

A Care Agency Built on Heart

Sowell Home Attendants was founded with a deep personal commitment to the people we serve. We’ve seen firsthand what quality home care means to a family — the relief it brings, the dignity it preserves, and the independence it restores.

We’re not a large, impersonal corporation. We’re a care agency where leadership is accessible, every client relationship is taken seriously, and caregivers are valued as the professionals they are.

Our Core Values

Compassion

🎗

Dignity

We preserve the independence, privacy, and self-worth of every person in our care. Dignity is foundational to everything we do.

🤝

Trust

⭐

Our Caregivers

The People Behind the Care

Our caregivers are the heart of Sowell Home Attendants — certified, trained professionals who chose home care as a calling, not just a job.

Before joining our team, every caregiver undergoes thorough vetting — criminal background checks, reference verification, skills assessments, and orientation training. We only bring on individuals we’d trust with our own families.

CPR and first aid certified
