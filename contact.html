Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can care begin?

In many cases we can arrange care within 24–48 hours of your initial consultation, including emergency placements.

What if I don’t like my caregiver?

A good match is important. If a caregiver isn’t the right fit, we’ll find someone who is — no questions asked.

Do you accept Medicaid or Medicare?

We accept private pay, long-term care insurance, and some Medicaid programs. Contact us to discuss your specific coverage.

Are caregivers employees or contractors?

Our caregivers are employees of Sowell Home Attendants — covered by our insurance, supervised, and held to our standards.

Can I get a consistent caregiver each visit?

Yes — we prioritize caregiver consistency so your loved one builds a comfortable, trusting relationship over time.

What areas do you serve?

We serve clients in our local community and surrounding areas. Contact us directly to confirm availability in your location.
